Tutoring Service

Tutoring is often timely, costly, and draining. I am a tutor at Khan's Tutorial but during quarantine will be doing one-on-one sessions with students by request. I will do my best to make tutoring an informtive, educating, and fun envirnment for individuals or all groups involved. Please set up a session by filling out the interest form below and filling in key information such as subjects, and letting me know the days and times when you are available.
