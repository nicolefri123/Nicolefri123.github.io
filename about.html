About Nicole

Hey Everyone!

I am a New Yorker, born and raised in the Bronx. I am currently a student and you can follow me on my journey throughout undergraduate and on my way to medical school. I love the arts, scientific research,debate, and writing about what I learned throughout life.

I recently graduated high school and am now joining the real world and I'm going to take it one day at a time. Here are some fun facts about my life:

  • I am African-American and my family's heritage is Ghanaian
  • I was born on Januaary 10th 2002 and am 18 years old
  • I have 5 siblings: 2 brothers and 3 sisters
  • I will be attending Hunter College as a Jenny Hunter Scholar with a major in Neuroscience and minor in Political science
  • I played Softball, Basketball, and Tennis throughout high school
  • In terms of extracurriculars, I was involved in Mock Trial, Black Student Union, Newspaper Team, National Honors Society, Camp Counseling, Tutoring and more
  • I'm terrified of pigeons, dogs, cats, squirrels, and bees but love and appreciate them from a distance
  • I love food, my favorite food combo is jollof with tuna salas
  • If you can't tell already, my favorite color is hot pink
  • My senior quote was "If they can make penicillin out of moldy bread, they can sure make something out of you," by Muhammad Ali, and I stand by that
